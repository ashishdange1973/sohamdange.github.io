About me

SOHAM is a 15-year old studying in 10th grade. What he has achieved is much more than his age and therefore, he is a Wunderkind. But, age doesn't matter when it is the smartness, etermination and hard work that play a vital role in deciding one's caliber.

Throughout the course of human history, there have been rare occasions where geniuses have sprouted out of the most unlikely of places. These young prodigious talents are often referred to as child prodigies or gifted children. These gifted children show high-performance capabilities both intellectually and academically at a very young age.

We have Soham Dange of Nagpur who has achieved lots of awards at very young age. Let's look at the wonderful journey of this young talent.

Journey of caterpillar to butterfly…

Every child has a different talent, interest, and skill. For a child it’s hard to discern the same and for Soham he is lucky that his parents could see his interests, encourage him in whatever he want to do and support him in any circumstances. Soham in his spare time loves to read books, play cricket, chess, table tennis and also plays bongo and piano. He wants to continue to take part in various competitions and face the challenges that are destined and achieve greater heights than the previous.

Soham, right from his Kindergarten days has taken part in various school events like Fancy Dress and School plays, which manifested to boost up his confidence and develop his oratorship. His father, Ashish and his Mother, Trupti would spend a lot of time for encouraging him and giving suitable environment to nurture the hidden talents in him.

Soham’s parents were the first to recognize that he has a special talent when they observed a spark of intellect in him. After he started taking honours in each and every field he participated, they recognised that they had a gifted child at home. They also realised that they needed to fully support and nurture his upbringing in order to unleash the full potential in him.

All his hard work while having fun with giving different types of competitive exams paid-off with succesion of honours in prestigious International Exams. Since childhood, he has been conferred with many accolades, with some of the momentous being : World rank 1 in Avogadro Chemistry exam, conducted by Waterloo University ; Securing Global Gold Rank 1 in VANDA : International Science Competition ; Bagging Gold medal and Country Rank 1 in Hong Kong International Mathematical Olympiad ; Bagging Gold medal and Country Rank 1 in Asia International Mathematical Olympiad ; Obtaining Silver medal in Philippine International Mathematics and Science Olympiads ; Procuring National Awards in Vidyarthi Vigyan Manthan Three times ; Qualifying for Indian National Junior Science Olympiad Two times ; Cracking Mathematical Association of America's - American Mathematical Competition and Achieving over 35 medals in Nationally Acknowledged exams. He is also selected for a Stanford ULO course for 2020-2021 Summer Term.

Let's wish many more accolades to this young talent in future and may his contributions benefit the community. At last but not least he wants to become a well Human being and serve the Nation and fulfil the dream of Almighty seen for him.
