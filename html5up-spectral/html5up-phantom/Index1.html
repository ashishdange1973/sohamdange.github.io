INTERNATIONAL ACHIVEMENTS

All his hard work while having fun with giving different types of competitive exams paid-off with succesion of honours in prestigious International Exams.

He seems to have countless prizes in his stuff and one of the most impressive one being World Rank One in the Avogadro Chemistry conducted by University of Waterloo, Canada in 2021.

Year 2021,has being a dream come true for him as he has been selected for ULO course at Stanford University , Qualified in the prestigious MAA-American Mathematical Competition(AMC),Bagged Gold Medal in Hong Kong International Mathematical Olympiad (HKIMO),Gold Medal in VANDA Science exam, Gold Medal in Asia International Mathematical Olympiad (AIMO), Exemplary Certificate in Singa Maths Contest, Silver medal in Singapore & Asian Schools Math Olympiad (SASMO).

*GIVEN BELOW ARE THE TESTIMONIALS OF SOHAM'S INTERNATIONAL ACHIEVEMENTS*

SASMO

Created in 2006, SASMO is one of largest math contests in Asia. It has expanded from 155 local participants in 2006 to more than 20 000 participants from 19 countries in SASMO 2016. More than 100 schools from Singapore took part in SASMO 2016..

VANDA

Vanda is an International Science Competition held annually around the globe. It focuses on the student’s ability to think critically and creatively to pick out hidden information to aid them in solving the question..

AMC

The MAA’s American Mathematics Competitions (MAA AMC) program leads the nation in strengthening the mathematical capabilities of the next generation of problem-solvers..

ANCQ

The ANCQ is a unique chemical education activity. It provides a major focus for secondary school students on the relevance of chemistry in an exciting and stimulating way. The ANCQ aims to promote interest in chemistry and the role of chemists in our society. .

Avogadro Chemistry

Chemistry is a diverse discipline, involving substances in every aspect of life – what we see, eat, smell, touch, and taste every day.

HKIMO

HKIMO, is named as Hong Kong International Mathematical Olympiad, is developed by former Hong Kong Mathematical Olympiad Team Team Leader Mr Wong Tin Chun.

SINGA MATHS

This is a new international Singapore Mathematics assessment for grades 1 to 11/12 written by top Singapore Ministry of Education (MOE) teachers that will offered to every country in the world.

AIMO

Asia International Mathematical Olympiad Union (AIMO Union) is a collaborative international organization among mathematical research organisations and individuals.

The Math Empire

Providing math platform to students. To understand, indulge, learn, compete.

DOKA Maths

DOKA (Depth of Knowledge Assessment) is an International Mathematical Problem-Solving Competition held annually around the globe. It is jointly organized by SMO Education Group, Academy TeamMathics, Singapore International Math Contests Centre (SIMCC), and supported by International Scholastic Trust (IST).

Math Kangaroo

Math Kangaroo (also known as International Mathematical Kangaroo) is an international mathematical competition where over 92 countries are represented.

Stanford University

Stanford Online offers learning opportunities via free online courses, online degrees, grad and professional certificates, e-learning, and open courses.
