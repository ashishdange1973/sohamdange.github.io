AIMO(Asia International Mathematical Olympiad)

Asia International Mathematical Olympiad Union (AIMO Union) is a collaborative international organization among mathematical research organisations and individuals.

It is the mission of AIMO Union to provide teenagers with an international platform for the purposes of learning Mathematics, cultural exchange, intriguing their mathematical insights, improving their qualities of scientific thinking minds and hence enhancing the friendship and cooperation of teenagers from different countries/regions.

Up to present, AIMO Union has already established Secretariats in countries such as Brazil, Bulgaria, China, Cambodia, Hong Kong, India, Indonesia, Kazakhstan, Macau, Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, Singapore, South Korea, Thailand and Turkey.

Since 2012, we have been holding AIMO Open together with worldwide top mathematical institutions. For the last years, AIMO Open has been successfully concluded (2012 Hong Kong, 2013 Taiwan, 2014 Chengdu, China, 2015 Thailand, 2016 Hong Kong, 2017 Malaysia, 2018 Thailand, 2019 Taiwan, 2020 Local). The number of students participating in AIMO Open keeps increasing.
