Champion Of Champions

As his name suggests, Soham is a allrounder with proclivity in mathematics and sciences. Since childhood, he has been conferred with many accolades, with some of the momentous being : World rank 1 in Avogadro Chemistry exam, conducted by Waterloo University ; Securing Global Gold Rank 1 in VANDA : International Science Competition ; Bagging Gold medal and Country Rank 1 in Hong Kong International Mathematical Olympiad ; Bagging Gold medal and Country Rank 1 in Asia International Mathematical Olympiad ; Obtaining Silver medal in Philippine International Mathematics and Science Olympiads ; Procuring National Awards in Vidyarthi Vigyan Manthan Three times ; Qualifying for Indian National Junior Science Olympiad Two times ; Cracking Mathematical Association of America's - American Mathematical Competition and Achieving over 35 medals in Nationally Acknowledged exams. He is also selected for a Stanford ULO course for 2020-2021 Summer Term.
